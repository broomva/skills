# 🚀 Deep-Dive Research Orchestrator Skill

Welcome! You have a production-ready research skill. Here's how to get started.

## What This Skill Does

Conducts comprehensive multi-dimensional research using **3 coordinated AI specialists**:

```
Your Research Request
      ↓
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│ Financial Analysis Agent    │  → Funding, revenue, valuation
├─────────────────────────────┤
│ Competitive Intelligence    │  → Team, tech, positioning
├─────────────────────────────┤
│ Product & Market Analysis   │  → Metrics, growth, engagement
└─────────────────────────────┘
      ↓
Professional Reports (15,000+ words)
```

**Output:** 5-7 professional markdown documents with full citations

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do I need to install the 3 dependencies?**
A: Yes! The skill won't work without financial-deep-research, competitor-intel, and app-store-optimization.

**Q: How long does research take?**
A: 2-3 hours for comprehensive research. Lightweight takes 1 hour.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: $0! All skills run locally using pre-installed tools.

**Q: Can I customize what gets researched?**
A: Absolutely! Scope, focus area, and output format all customizable.

**Q: What can I research with this?**
A: Companies, products, markets, technologies - anything publicly known.

**Q: How many sources are used?**
A: 20+ verified sources minimum per research project.

**Q: Can I share the reports?**
A: Yes! Reports are professional-grade and ready for stakeholders.
